Challenges in Open Source & Our Transformative Approach

Addressing Barriers to Diversity, Innovation, and Contribution in the Open Source Ecosystem

Core Problem

The open-source community faces significant challenges in attracting and retaining diverse talent, especially non-technical individuals. One major barrier is the lack of a clear roadmap for non-technical contributors to meaningfully engage with open-source projects. Additionally, there’s a growing demand for new open-source solutions, yet our ecosystem struggles with a shortage of innovation, particularly among startups and emerging ventures.

Our Solution

The IDB Accelerator Program addresses these gaps by offering a structured environment where both technical and non-technical participants can collaborate, guided by mentorship and practical experience. By providing the tools, resources, and support needed, we empower diverse talents to contribute to and drive innovation in the open-source ecosystem, ultimately shaping its future.

Empowering the Next Billion Curators to Build the Future of Open Source

The IDB Accelerator Program, launched by Open Source Community Africa (OSCA), was born out of a deep-seated passion for democratizing technology and making open-source more accessible. At the heart of our initiative is the belief that innovation should be open, collaborative, and inclusive.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower individuals from all backgrounds to innovate within the open-source ecosystem. We foster collaboration between technical and non-technical individuals, emphasising mentorship, community, and practical experience.

Our Vision

Our vision is to establish the IDB Accelerator as the premier hub for nurturing exceptional talent across various technology roles, transforming open-source collaboration into groundbreaking innovations.

Why Join The IDB Accelerator?

01

Hands-on Work Experience

Collaborate with teams on real-world open-source projects, gaining practical experience that you can showcase to future employers or investors.

02

Mentorship from Industry Experts

You’ll receive personalised guidance from experienced mentors who are leaders in the open-source space. Their feedback will help shape your project and equip you with the skills to excel, whether you’re coding, designing, or managing projects.

03

Access to Networking Opportunities

Connect with tech leaders, potential investors, and like-minded innovators through exclusive events, workshops, and community meet ups. These connections can be key to unlocking future job offers, collaborations, and partnerships.

04

Resources to Ensure Success

Participants receive essential resources to ensure that limitations don’t hinder your success. We are committed to fostering equitable access to technology.
